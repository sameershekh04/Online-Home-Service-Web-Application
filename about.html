Our Journey

As 3rd year BSCIT students, we combined our passion for technology with our academic knowledge to embark on this exciting journey. What started as a college project quickly evolved into a full-fledged startup idea.

Our diverse skill sets complement each other perfectly - Sameer's vision, frontend and backend abilities, Abhay's backend and database expertise, and Shashank's creative frontend abilities create a well-rounded team capable of bringing any idea to life.

We're not just building a product; we're gaining real-world experience while still pursuing our education. This balance of learning and doing is what drives us to innovate and push boundaries every day.
